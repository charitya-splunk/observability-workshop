RUM Detectors

Let’s say we want to know about an issue in production without waiting for a ticket from our support center. This is where creating detectors in RUM will be helpful for us.

  1. Go to the RUM view of the Syncreator App. Scroll to the LCP chart, click the chart menu icon, and click Create Detector.

  2. Rename the test to include your team name and initials, and change the scope of the detector to App so we are not limited to a single URL or page. Change the threshold and sensitivity until there is at least one alert event in the time frame.

  3. Change the alert severity and add a recipient if you’d like, and click Activate to save the Detector.
